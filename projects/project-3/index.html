INTERNET FORAGING

Edible, Poisonous, or Fake? Test your skills here!

In the wake of AI, distiguishing 'real' from 'fake' becomes another daily part of parsing through the Internet. I was stricken to how similar this process was to plant identification while foraging — one misstep means death. Although certainly not as consequential, the meticulous inspection of every image reminded me of how carefully classified plants are.

AI generated images have a particular effect on documentation of scientific artifacts — they, of course, are not an accurate representation of reality, yet pose to be so. As these images become more convincing, it'll become more and more difficult to find accurate documentation of certain specimens as search pages are flooded with generated images rather than photos. Curious, I was motivated to try and see if AI images are convincing enough to fool people.

I encourage visitors to try and eat from this garden I've provided. Who knows? You might find something tasty...or not.
